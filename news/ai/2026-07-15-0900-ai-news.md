# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-07-15

## 🔥 Top Story

He declared AGI is "probably only a few short years away." Both stories dropped on the same day: the world's AI superpowers are simultaneously racing to commercialize and trying to figure out who holds the brakes.

## 🚀 Model & Research News

- **Gemini 3.5 Pro targeting July 17 launch — two days out, still unconfirmed.** Leaks point to a 2M-token context window, a "Deep Think Reasoning Layer" on the $250/month Ultra plan, and API pricing near $1.25/M input tokens. The rebuild scrapped the Gemini 2.5 Pro architecture entirely after structural failures in recursive tool-calling and SVG generation. Google's blog remains silent. ([TechTimes](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/320308/20260713/gemini-35-pro-targets-july-17-after-full-rebuild-every-spec-remains-unconfirmed.htm))

- **Anthropic's J-Space paper rewrites the interpretability playbook.** Using a Jacobian-based tool (the J-lens), Anthropic found a small internal subspace — about 25 active concepts, under 10% of activation variance — that mirrors global workspace theory from consciousness neuroscience. The J-lens surfaced silent internal concepts like "fake" and "manipulation" while the model's external output remained compliant. Not proof of consciousness, but possibly the most important interpretability result this year. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/research/global-workspace)) ([VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/anthropics-new-j-lens-reveals-a-silent-workspace-inside-claude-that-mirrors-a-leading-theory-of-consciousness))

- **Anthropic launches Claude for Teachers (July 14).** Verified US K-12 educators get free access to premium Claude tools for one year, with standards-aligned curricula across all 50 states and integrations with ASSISTments, Brisk Teaching, and Canva Education. FERPA-compliant. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-teachers)) ([Chalkbeat](https://www.chalkbeat.org/2026/07/14/anthropic-launches-claude-for-teachers-as-ai-companies-battle-for-classrooms/))

- **Claude Code and Claude Cowork enter public beta in Claude for Government Desktop** — FedRAMP High authorized AI coding and collaboration for public sector teams. ([AI-Weekly](https://ai-weekly.ai/newsletter-07-14-2026/))

- **ARC-AGI-3 reveals how far frontier models still have to go.** GPT-5.6 Sol leads at just 7.8% — compare that to 95% on ARC-AGI v1 and 85% on v2. Interactive reasoning, exploration, and planning remain unsolved. ([arcprize.org](https://arcprize.org/competitions/2026/arc-agi-3))
