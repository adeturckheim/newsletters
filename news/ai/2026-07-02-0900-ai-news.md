# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-07-02

## 🔥 Top Story

> **Anthropic launches Claude Sonnet 5 and makes it the default for all users — the first mid-tier model to beat its Opus sibling on a major coding benchmark.** Released June 30 and rolled out as the default model for every Free and Pro user on July 1, Sonnet 5 scores 80.4% on Terminal-Bench 2.1 vs. Opus 4.8's 74.6%, while coming within striking distance on SWE-bench Pro (63.2% vs. 69.2%). Introductory pricing is aggressive at $2/$10 per million tokens (input/output) through August 31 — cheaper than Sonnet 4.6. Anthropic is positioning this as the go-to model for agentic workloads, with dramatically improved prompt-injection resistance (attack success drops from 31.5% to 0.93% in browser-use scenarios). ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/30/anthropic-launches-claude-sonnet-5-as-a-cheaper-way-to-run-agents/)) ([DataCamp](https://www.datacamp.com/blog/claude-sonnet-5))

## 🚀 Model & Research News

- **Fable 5 is back online globally after 19-day export ban.** The US Department of Commerce lifted export controls on Anthropic's Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 as of July 1. Anthropic deployed a new classifier that blocks the disputed jailbreak technique in over 99% of cases. Fable 5 is included at up to 50% of weekly usage limits for Pro/Max/Team plans through July 7. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/anthropic-says-trump-admin-has-lifted-export-controls-on-claude-fable-5-and-mythos-5.html)) ([MacRumors](https://www.macrumors.com/2026/07/01/anthropic-fable-5-relaunch/))

- **Google drops Nano Banana 2 Lite (Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite Image) — 4-second image generation at $0.034 per 1,000 images.** Launched June 30, this is Google's fastest and cheapest image generation model, optimized for high-throughput enterprise workflows rather than artistic quality. Available via Google AI Studio and the Gemini API. ([VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/google-unveils-nano-banana-2-lite-aka-gemini-3-1-flash-lite-for-low-cost-4-second-fast-enterprise-image-generations/))

- **xAI rolls out Grok 4.1 to all users.** Available now on grok.com, X, and mobile apps in Auto mode. xAI also introduced `/goal` in Grok Build — a long-running autonomous mode for larger implementation tasks with plan, execute, pause, and resume controls. ([xAI](https://x.ai/news))

- **Google Search gets its biggest upgrade in 25 years — AI agents powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash.** AI Mode now has 1B+ monthly users globally. Users can create, customize, and manage AI "information agents" that monitor topics 24/7 and take actions like tracking flights or organizing emails. ([Google Blog](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/search/search-io-2026/))
