# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-04-22

## 🔥 Top Story

> **Jeff Bezos nears $10B funding round for Project Prometheus, his stealth AI lab.** Bloomberg reports Bezos is close to finalizing a massive round for his AI startup focused on models that understand the physical world — think robotics, spatial reasoning, and embodied intelligence. If closed, it would be one of the largest individual-led AI funding rounds ever and signals Bezos is betting big on physical AI as the next frontier, not just language models. ([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-21/jeff-bezos-nears-10-billion-funding-round-for-ai-lab-ft-says))

## 🚀 Model & Research News

- **Claude Opus 4.7 completing its first full week in the wild**: Released April 16, Anthropic's latest flagship shows substantial gains in advanced software engineering (especially on the hardest tasks), first-ever high-res image support in a Claude model (up to 2576px / 3.75MP), and a new ability to devise ways to verify its own outputs. Pricing unchanged at $5/$25 per M tokens. Already available on AWS Bedrock and GitHub. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-7)) ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/16/anthropic-claude-opus-4-7-model-mythos.html))
- **Moonshot AI drops Kimi K2.6 open-source**: New open-source model with strong tool-use (54.0), SWE-Bench Pro (58.6), and SWE-bench Multilingual (76.7) scores. Ships in Instant, Thinking, Agent, and Agent Swarm variants — the swarm variant is particularly interesting for multi-agent workflows. ([LLM Stats](https://llm-stats.com/ai-news))
- **Google unveils next-gen TPUs at Cloud Next**: Announced April 21 at Google Cloud Next (running April 22-24), Google's new Tensor Processing Units are designed to handle the compute demands of Gemini 3.1 and beyond. dbt Labs is also showcasing AI-ready features at the same event. ([TechStartups](https://techstartups.com/2026/04/21/top-tech-news-today-april-21-2026/))
- **Anthropic's Project Glasswing goes live for defensive cybersecurity**: Using the withheld Claude Mythos model, Anthropic partnered with Amazon, Microsoft, Apple, Google, and Nvidia to provide up to $100M in usage credits for defensive cybersecurity. Mythos was kept from public release after it independently identified thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities. ([MIT Technology Review](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/04/13/1135675/want-to-understand-the-current-state-of-ai-check-out-these-charts/))
- **AI-quantum convergence accelerates**: TIME reports Google and Oratomic research suggests quantum computers capable of breaking internet encryption may arrive sooner than expected, with AI helping accelerate the progress. ([TIME](https://time.com/article/2026/04/07/ai-quantum-computing-advance/))
