# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-06-23

## 🔥 Top Story

> **Google DeepMind bets $75M on Hollywood with a first-of-its-kind A24 partnership.** Google is investing roughly $75 million to co-develop AI-powered filmmaking tools with indie studio A24 — think AI-generated storyboards and new production workflows, not prompted video slop. A24 keeps full control of its content library, and the studio says the tools will "preserve creative control and support risk-taking." It's the clearest signal yet that frontier AI labs see creative industries as the next major application layer. ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/22/google-deepmind-bets-75m-on-ais-future-in-hollywood-with-a24-deal/)) ([Variety](https://variety.com/2026/film/news/google-a24-ai-filmmaking-tools-1236787297/))

([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/fable-mythos-access)) ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/06/13/anthropic-disables-fable-mythos-export-controls-national-security-threat/))

Initial results: 64 PRs filed, 37 patches already merged across 19 projects including cURL, Go, Python, and Sigstore. Participating maintainers get ChatGPT Pro, Codex Security access, and API credits. ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/patch-the-planet/)) ([Trail of Bits](https://blog.trailofbits.com/2026/06/22/introducing-patch-the-planet/))

Prediction markets are at roughly 50-55% for a public launch by month-end. Meanwhile, Gemini 3.5 Flash has been GA since May 19 at $1.50/$9.00 per million tokens. ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/22/the-ai-world-is-getting-loopy/))

## 🛠️ Tools & Developer Updates

- **"Loop engineering" goes mainstream.** Claude Code creator Boris Cherny at Meta's @Scale conference declared loops "as big a step as the transition from source code to agents." Peter Steinberger (OpenAI) and Addy Osmani (Google Cloud) co-coined the term — it's the practice of designing AI agent cycles that self-correct against verifiable criteria (tests pass, lint is clean) and retry until success or budget exhaustion. TechCrunch ran a full feature calling the AI world "loopy." ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/22/the-ai-world-is-getting-loopy/)) ([Medium](https://medium.com/mountain-movers/what-a-loop-actually-is-boris-chernys-three-stage-definition-33dd2bfe01b3))
