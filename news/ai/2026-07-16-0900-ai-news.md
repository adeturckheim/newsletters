# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-07-16

## 🔥 Top Story

> **China's AI companion law took effect yesterday, forcing ByteDance's Doubao and Alibaba's Qwen to shut down personalized AI agents overnight — hundreds of millions of users woke up to features switched off.** The [Interim Measures for AI Human-Like Interaction Services](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/320525/20260715/china-ai-companion-law-takes-effect-doubao-qwen-shut-down-millions-lose-chat-data.htm), published by five Chinese agencies in April, [formally took effect July 15](https://foreignpolicy.com/2026/07/14/china-ai-companions-regulation-takes-effect/). Providers must now disclose AI isn't human, add anti-addiction reminders, include self-harm crisis pathways, and are barred from offering virtual companion services to minors. It's the most aggressive regulation of AI emotional engagement anywhere in the world — and a template other governments are watching closely.

## 🚀 Model & Research News

- **Gemini 3.5 Pro reportedly targeting July 17 GA launch.** Google's rebuilt flagship aims for a [2M-token context window](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/320308/20260713/gemini-35-pro-targets-july-17-after-full-rebuild-every-spec-remains-unconfirmed.htm) (the largest of any production frontier model), a Deep Think reasoning layer, and autonomous workflow capabilities. Google has still not officially confirmed the date or specs. If it lands tomorrow, expect a benchmark shakeup.

- **Future of Life Institute releases 2026 AI Safety Index — nobody passes.** Anthropic earned the [highest grade at C+](https://unrot.co/blogs/today-top-10-ai-news-july-15-2026), with OpenAI and Google DeepMind at C, Meta at D+, and xAI, DeepSeek, and Mistral effectively failing. The report card highlights that even the most safety-conscious labs have major gaps.

- **ARC-AGI-3 scores confirm frontier models still can't do interactive reasoning.** GPT-5.6 Sol leads at just [7.8%](https://benchlm.ai/benchmarks/arcAgi3) — a humbling result given that v1 is at 95% and v2 at 85%. Terra scored 0.8%, Luna 0.2%. The benchmark evaluates whether agents can learn unfamiliar task mechanics through action and feedback — the kind of flexible learning humans do effortlessly.

- **Andrej Karpathy reportedly joins Anthropic.** The former Tesla AI director and OpenAI founding member has [joined Anthropic's ranks](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-news-today-july-15-2026), along with Tom Blomfield (Monzo co-founder) who is joining the AI compute team. A major talent acquisition if confirmed.

- **South Korea unveils $880B decade-long AI infrastructure plan.** President Lee Jae-myung [announced 1,350 trillion won](https://unrot.co/blogs/today-top-10-ai-news-july-15-2026) in spending — ~$518B for memory chip manufacturing, ~$550B for AI data centers, targeting 8.4GW of data center capacity by 2029 and growing humanoid robotics market share from 1% to 20% by 2028.
