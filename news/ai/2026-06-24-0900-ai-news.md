# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-06-24

## 🔥 Top Story

> **Anthropic launches Claude Tag — an always-on AI teammate that lives in your Slack channels.** Announced yesterday, Claude Tag (@Claude) is a shared, persistent AI identity that any team member can summon, hand tasks to, and pick up mid-conversation. It breaks requests into stages, works through them autonomously, and — with "ambient" mode enabled — proactively flags forgotten threads and surfaces relevant info across channels. Anthropic says 65% of its own product team's code is written by their internal version. Available now in research preview for Claude Enterprise and Team customers. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-claude-tag)) ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/23/anthropics-claude-tag-is-learning-your-company-one-slack-message-at-a-time/)) ([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-23/anthropic-wants-claude-to-be-your-new-slack-coworker))

## 🚀 Model & Research News

- **Google DeepMind's brain drain sends GOOGL tumbling 7%.** In the span of five days, Google lost Nobel laureate John Jumper (AlphaFold) to Anthropic and Transformer co-inventor Noam Shazeer to OpenAI. Alphabet had its worst single-day drop in over a year on Monday. Analysts are questioning whether Google's internal bureaucracy is pushing top talent toward the pre-IPO upside at rivals. ([Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/06/23/ai-lab-agi-google-deepmind-departures)) ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/22/alphabet-goog-stock-ai-departures.html)) ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/06/23/google-deepmind-ai-researcher-departures-raise-doubts-about-ability-to-win-the-ai-race-shazeer-jumper-eye-on-ai/))
- **Fable 5 / Mythos 5 still offline — day 12 of the US export ban.** No restoration timeline. Anthropic's status page shows both models remain suspended following the June 12 government directive citing a narrow Fable 5 jailbreak. Anthropic disagrees with the standard, saying it would "essentially halt all new model deployments for all frontier model providers." Claude Opus 4.8 remains unaffected and holds #1 on the Arena leaderboard. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/fable-mythos-access)) ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/06/13/anthropic-disables-fable-mythos-export-controls-national-security-threat/))
- **Gemini 3.5 Pro still MIA with 6 days left on Google's June deadline.** Prediction markets sit at roughly 50-55% for a public launch by June 30. The model is enterprise-only on Vertex AI. Sundar Pichai's I/O promise of "give us until next month" is looking increasingly strained. ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/22/the-ai-world-is-getting-loopy/)) ([Swisher Post](https://www.swisherpost.com/technology/gemini-3-5-pro-june-launch-delay/))
- **Arena leaderboard (6.9M votes, 367 models):** Claude Opus 4.8 holds #1 at ~1510 ELO, followed by GPT-5.5 Pro, Gemini 3.1 Pro Preview, Claude Opus 4.7, and GPT-5.5. Top five clustered within ~55 ELO points — tightest spread on record. ([Arena.ai](https://arena.ai/leaderboard/text))

## 🛠️ Tools & Developer Updates

- **Claude Tag redefines "AI coworker."** Beyond the headline product, the enterprise features are notable: admins can scope tools, memories, and token budgets per channel; Claude learns org context over time so teams don't re-explain from scratch. Auto-migration for existing Slack integrations happens August 3. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-claude-tag)) ([Neowin](https://www.neowin.net/news/anthropic-introduces-claude-tag-a-new-ai-teammate-for-slack/))
- **"Tokenmaxxing" officially dead.** The enterprise AI spending backlash continues: Uber burned its entire 2026 AI budget in four months (largely on Claude Code), imposed a $1,500/month/employee cap. Microsoft cancelled Claude Code licenses for several product divisions. Meta killed its internal token leaderboard. Uber's COO: "That link [between token usage and consumer innovation] is not there yet." ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/05/26/uber-coo-ai-spending-tokens-claude-code/)) ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/02/uber-caps-employee-ai-spending-after-blowing-through-budget-in-four-months/))

## 💰 Funding & Business

- **The AI IPO supercycle is here.** Anthropic filed its S-1 confidentially on June 1 (post-$65B Series H at $965B valuation). OpenAI filed June 8, targeting up to $1T at IPO. SpaceX began trading June 12 after the largest IPO on record ($75B raised). These three could be the largest IPOs ever recorded. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/01/anthropic-ipo-s1-prospectus.html)) ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/08/openai-confidentially-files-for-ipo-prepping-wall-street-for-ai-debut.html))
- **Anthropic paying SpaceX $1.25B/month for Colossus compute.** The deal gives Anthropic access to ~325,000 NVIDIA GPUs across Colossus I and II through May 2029 — up to $45B over the full term. Grok's decline left SpaceX's data centers at ~11% utilization; Anthropic fills the gap. ([Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/05/20/anthropic-spacex-compute))
- **OpenAI generating $2B/month in revenue** but forecasting a $14B loss for 2026 (adjusted operating margin: -122%). Enterprise now 40%+ of revenue. Sam Altman disclosed that Anthropic has overtaken OpenAI in corporate AI spending among enterprise customers — a first. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/08/openai-confidentially-files-for-ipo-prepping-wall-street-for-ai-debut.html))
