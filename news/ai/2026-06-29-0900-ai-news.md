# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-06-29

## 🔥 Top Story

> **OpenAI previews GPT-5.6 Sol, Terra, and Luna — but only ~20 companies can use it.** On June 26, OpenAI unveiled its next-generation model family in a limited preview gated by U.S. government approval. Sol, the flagship, sets a new state of the art on Terminal-Bench 2.1 for command-line workflows and is OpenAI's strongest cybersecurity model yet. A new "ultra" mode leverages subagents for complex tasks. But here's the catch: under the Trump administration's June 2 executive order requiring pre-release national security reviews, GPT-5.6 is locked to roughly 20 pre-approved partners. General availability is "coming weeks" — if the government signs off. ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/previewing-gpt-5-6-sol/)) ([Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/06/26/openai-gpt-sol-terra-luna-trump)) ([MacRumors](https://www.macrumors.com/2026/06/26/openai-gpt-5-6-sol/))

## 🚀 Model & Research News

- **Grok 4.5 enters private beta at SpaceX and Tesla.** Elon Musk announced on June 28 that xAI's latest model — 1.5 trillion parameters on the V9 foundation — is being tested internally. It integrates Cursor training data following SpaceX's $60B acquisition of Anysphere. Musk claims performance is "close to or exceeding" Claude Opus. Plans call for new models trained from scratch monthly through year-end. ([CryptoBriefing](https://cryptobriefing.com/grok-4-5-private-beta-spacex-tesla/)) ([SeekingAlpha](https://seekingalpha.com/news/4607894-elon-musk-says-grok-4_5-enters-private-testing-at-spacex-and-tesla))

- **Mythos 5 partially restored for U.S. critical infrastructure.** On June 27, Anthropic received government clearance to redeploy Mythos 5 to 100+ trusted U.S. organizations including Fortune 500 firms and Project Glasswing participants. Fable 5 remains offline — day 17 of the export ban — with no timeline for restoration. ([Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/2026/06/27/anthropic-cleared-to-restore-mythos-5-access-to-certain-us-organisations)) ([CybersecurityNews](https://cybersecuritynews.com/anthropic-claude-mythos-5/))

- **Chinese frontier wave hits Hugging Face.** Six competitive releases in two weeks — Qwen 3.7, DeepSeek V4.1, Hunyuan Large 3, ERNIE 5.1, Doubao Pro, and GLM-6. DeepSeek V4.1 Flash took the top trending slot within a week of release. Chinese open-weight models now hold five of the top ten download slots, the highest concentration on record. ([Presenc AI](https://presenc.ai/research/huggingface-trending-models-june-2026))

- **OpenAI and Broadcom unveiled Jalapeño.** OpenAI's first custom inference chip, designed in just nine months with AI assistance, targets ~50% lower inference cost per token vs. current Nvidia GPUs. Built by TSMC, deployment begins late 2026. ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/24/openai-and-broadcom-reveal-jalapeno-first-ai-chip-in-partnership.html)) ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/24/openai-unveils-its-first-custom-chip-built-by-broadcom/))
