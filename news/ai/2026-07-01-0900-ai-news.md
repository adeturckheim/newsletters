# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-07-01

## 🔥 Top Story

> **Fable 5 is back — Anthropic redeploys its most powerful model today after 19-day US government ban.** Starting July 1, Claude Fable 5 returns globally on Claude.ai, Claude Code, Claude Cowork, and the API. The export controls imposed on June 12 — triggered by a reported jailbreak vulnerability — have been lifted after Anthropic worked with the Trump administration to implement new classifiers that block the exploit 93% of the time. When Fable's classifiers detect sensitive requests (cybersecurity, biology/chemistry, distillation), the response is automatically routed to Claude Opus 4.8 instead. Anthropic also announced a new industry-wide jailbreak framework developed with Amazon, Microsoft, Google, and other Glasswing partners. For Pro/Max/Team plans, Fable 5 is included at up to 50% of weekly usage limits through July 7. ([Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/redeploying-fable-5)) ([Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-30/us-government-lifts-restrictions-on-anthropic-s-fable-5-model)) ([CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/30/anthropic-says-trump-admin-has-lifted-export-controls-on-claude-fable-5-and-mythos-5.html))

## 🚀 Model & Research News

- **OpenAI launches GeneBench-Pro — a new benchmark exposing that top AI fails real biology 70% of the time.** Released June 30, the benchmark presents 129 synthetic problems across genomics, quantitative biology, and translational medicine. GPT-5.6 Sol tops the leaderboard at 31.5% (Pro mode), up from <5% for GPT-5. Claude Opus 4.8 reaches 16.0%. OpenAI frames it as measuring "research taste" — the chain of judgment calls that shape scientific analysis. ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-genebench-pro/)) ([AlphaSignal](https://alphasignal.ai/news/openai-s-genebench-pro-exposes-that-top-ai-fails-real-biology-70-of-the-time))

- **GPT-5.6 Sol approaching general availability — July deployment on Cerebras at 750 tokens/sec.** Still limited to ~20 government-vetted organizations since the June 26 preview, but OpenAI is now planning broad API access in July. The model includes "ultra" mode with subagent orchestration and a new max reasoning tier. ([OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/previewing-gpt-5-6-sol/)) ([VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/openai-unveils-gpt-5-6-sol-terra-and-luna-models-but-only-accessible-to-limited-preview-partners-for-now-per-us-gov))
