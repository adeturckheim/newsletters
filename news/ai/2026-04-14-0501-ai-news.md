([NVIDIA GEAR](https://research.nvidia.com/labs/gear/)) ([the-decoder](https://the-decoder.com/nvidia-researcher-jim-fan-expects-gpt-3-moment-for-robotics-in-the-next-few-years/))

The "Android of robotics" strategy keeps widening. ([NVIDIA Newsroom](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-announces-open-physical-ai-data-factory-blueprint-to-accelerate-robotics-vision-ai-agents-and-autonomous-vehicle-development))
