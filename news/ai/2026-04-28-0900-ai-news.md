# 🗞️ AI Daily Briefing — 2026-04-28

## 🔥 Top Story

> **China blocks Meta's $2 billion acquisition of Manus.** Beijing's National Development and Reform Commission [ordered the deal's cancellation](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/27/meta-manus-china-blocks-acquisition-ai-startup.html), invoking export control and technology transfer laws. Meta acquired the Chinese-founded AI agent startup in December 2025 and had already integrated its tech internally — unwinding the deal will be messy. Two Manus co-founders have been banned from leaving China. This is Xi testing extraterritorial reach over AI assets in a deepening US-China tech war, and it sets a precedent for every cross-border AI acquisition going forward.

## 🚀 Model & Research News

- **[OpenAI ships GPT-5.5](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-5/)**: Released April 23, GPT-5.5 is rolling out to Plus, Pro, Business, and Enterprise users. OpenAI calls it their "smartest model yet" — matches GPT-5.4 latency while using fewer tokens per task. Available in the API as GPT-5.5 and GPT-5.5 Pro.

- **[DeepSeek drops V4 in preview](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/24/deepseek-v4-llm-preview-open-source-ai-competition-china.html)**: V4-Pro (1.6T params, 49B active) and V4-Flash (284B params, 13B active), both with 1M context. Open source, open weights. [V4-Pro beats all open models on math and coding](https://www.technologyreview.com/2026/04/24/1136422/why-deepseeks-v4-matters/) and trails only Gemini 3.1 Pro on world knowledge. DeepSeek also slashed API cache-hit pricing to 1/10th.

- **[Claude Opus 4.7 dominates LMArena](https://arena.ai/leaderboard/)**: Sitting at 1504 Elo — 30+ points ahead of the field. With 5.9M votes across 347 models, it's the clearest frontrunner the arena has seen.

- **[Anthropic unveils Claude Mythos Preview](https://llm-stats.com/ai-news)**: A new model with advanced cybersecurity capabilities, released alongside the Opus 4.7 wave.

- **[AI Scientist-v2 paper accepted at major conference](https://www.devflokers.com/blog/new-ai-papers-arxiv-last-24-hours-april-2026)**: The first fully AI-generated paper accepted at a top venue — an agentic tree-search system that autonomously proposes hypotheses, runs experiments, and writes papers. A milestone for automated scientific discovery.

## 🛠️ Tools & Developer Updates

- **[SpaceX swoops in on Cursor with $60B deal](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/22/how-spacex-preempted-a-2b-fundraise-with-a-60b-buyout-offer/)**: Cursor was raising $2B at a $50B valuation (a16z, Thrive, Nvidia) when SpaceX offered a $10B collaboration fee and a path to $60B acquisition. Prediction markets say the option gets exercised. The AI coding tool built by four MIT dropouts just became the most valuable developer tool company ever.

- **[LMDeploy vulnerability exploited within 13 hours](https://news.ycombinator.com/front)**: CVE-2026-33626, a server-side request forgery flaw in the popular open-source LLM deployment toolkit, came under active exploitation almost immediately after disclosure. Patch now if you're running it.

- **[Anthropic working on Bugcrawl for Claude Code](https://news.ycombinator.com/front)**: A new feature that scans repositories for bugs using 10 parallel agents — likely targeting Teams and Enterprise plans.
